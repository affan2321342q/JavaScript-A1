Welcome to SIT Nagpur

Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Nagpur, is committed to providing high-quality education and fostering innovation, creativity, and excellence. Explore our college, learn about our departments, and get to know our students.

About Our College

SIT Nagpur is one of the leading institutes for higher education in technology and sciences. The college campus offers world-class facilities, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a vibrant environment for students to excel in both academics and extracurricular activities.

Our vision is to create professionals who are not only technically skilled but also socially responsible. With a strong emphasis on research and innovation, SIT Nagpur is shaping future leaders in engineering and technology.

The institute also promotes cultural diversity and holistic development through student clubs, events, and initiatives aimed at nurturing creativity, teamwork, and leadership skills.

About Our Department

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at SIT Nagpur. Our department focuses on cutting-edge research, innovation, and quality education. Students here get opportunities to explore Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, and other advanced fields of Computer Science.
